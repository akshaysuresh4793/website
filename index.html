Financial comment automation

Gen AI for claims Operational Analytics CVS Health · 2025
Automates recommendations for up to ~200K financial comments / year

Context

Financial comments are contract clauses with free-text instructions for processing claims.

Job to be done

How might we apply those instructions to a claim and produce a recommendation?

Methodology

  • KAG (Knowledge Augmented Generation) for enterprise concept vectors
  • NER on financial comments to tag concepts
  • Decision flows for how concepts interact

Outcome

  • Live claims pipeline that generates recommendations
  • Bot applies recommendations to adjudicate claims
  • ~200K financial comments automated annually
